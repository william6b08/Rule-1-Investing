Principle #1: Business is Easily Understood

Find a business that is much easier for you to understand is the key to become a successful investor. It is your money, this is why it is crucial that you retain full control of the investment. One way to do so is by investing into a business/ industry you understand.

Cricle of Competence

Your circle of competence makes up of 3 separate circles, the section that is overlapped is your area of competence. Try to find them and you will get a better idea of what your investment direction would be.

Understand what the company is doing

It is important to understand what the company's future plan is in order to have a better understanding of its performance in a long run. One way to find out about this is by reading the 10-K company submitted to U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission. Enlisted companies are required to submit 10-K form every year to report their financial data of that fiscal year. In the section Business, company will provide a rundown of what kind of company it is, what they accomplished this year, acknowledgement of any challenges they are facing and future plan. All in all, 10-K is a great way to start your digging into the companies you want to invest into.
